5. The Eighty Six

Heat 20
NYC · Eater

At 86 Bedford Street in the West Village — the storied former home of Chumley’s — The Eighty Six is the latest from Tilman Fertitta, Eugene Remm, and Catch Hospitality Group. The team is also behind Corner Store , the celebrity magnet in Soho. The 35-seat steakhouse, which opened in September 2025, swaps the Frog Club’s froggy decor for velvet banquettes, geometric marble floors, and literary nods from Rockwell Group. Chef Michael Vignola’s menu moves from oysters with gin-celery mignonette and horseradish-snow shrimp to 30-day-aged rib-eyes and a cheesesteak. Cocktails are theatrical — smoked dirty martinis, fat-washed pours, fluffy margaritas.

7. Odo East Village

Heat 20
NYC · Eater

Two-Michelin-starred Japanese kaiseki Odo spun off with Odo East Village in February, its comparatively casual restaurant with an elegant counter-seat space. It focuses on what chef Hiroki Odo and the team are calling “ kaiseki izakaya,” an a la carte menu with Japanese raw, grilled, simmered, steamed, and fried dishes.
